Healthy Food — Quick Guide

Healthy food helps our body stay active, strong, and energetic. Here is a quick guide using lists!

Steps for Healthy Living:

  1. Drink plenty of water every day
  2. Eat fresh fruits and vegetables
  3. Exercise regularly
  4. Get enough sleep at night
  5. Avoid too much junk food

Healthy Foods:

  • Apples — rich in vitamins
  • Milk — helps build strong bones
  • Carrots — good for eyesight
  • Rice — gives energy to the body

Key Terms:

Nutrition
Nutrients needed for healthy growth
Exercise
Physical activity that keeps the body fit
Balanced Diet
Eating the right amount of healthy foods
